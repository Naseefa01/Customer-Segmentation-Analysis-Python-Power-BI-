# Customer-Segmentation-Analysis-Python-Power-BI-

## Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to understand different types of customers and their purchasing behavior.
This project aims to segment customers based on their spending patterns to help improve targeting and decision-making.

---

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer data using Python and Power BI to classify customers into different segments based on their value.
The segmentation helps identify high-value customers and understand distribution across different customer groups.

---

## Objectives

- Segment customers into different value categories
- Identify high-value, medium-value, and low-value customers
- Analyze revenue contribution across different customer segments
- Identify top-performing customers based on total purchase amount
- Understand revenue distribution across product categories
- Build an interactive dashboard for visualization

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Python – Data cleaning and segmentation
- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

---

## Key Metrics

## Key Features of Dashboard

-  Customer Segmentation Distribution
-  Customer Count by Category
-  Segment-wise Comparison
-  Interactive Slicers

---

## Key Insights

- Majority of customers fall under the low-value segment
- A small percentage of customers contribute as high-value customers
- Medium-value customers represent a transition group with growth potential
- High-value customers are limited but highly contribute to revenue
- Medium value customers generate the highest overall revenue
- Customer distribution across gender is fairly balanced
- Revenue is highest in categories like Clothing and shoes compared to others

---
## Recommendations

- Focus on retaining high-value customers through personalized offers
- Convert medium-value customers into high-value through targeted strategies
- Improve engagement strategies for low-value customers
- Use segmentation for better marketing and decision-making
